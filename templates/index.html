FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our resume analyzer and how scoring works.

Q
What is an ATS and why does it matter?

An Applicant Tracking System filters resumes before a human ever sees them. Up to 75% of resumes are rejected automatically. Our tool reverse-engineers ATS behavior so your resume passes the first gate.

Q
What is a good resume score?

A score above 80 indicates a well-optimized resume. However, the score is a guide — key sections like contact info and experience must be flawless regardless of the overall number.

Q
Can ATS read PDF files?

Yes. Modern ATS platforms handle PDFs well, and PDFs are actually preferred since they preserve formatting and can't be accidentally edited. We recommend submitting your resume as a PDF.

Q
Is my resume data kept private?

Your resume is processed in memory for analysis and is never stored on our servers or shared with third parties. Each session is completely isolated and private.

Q
How do I improve my score quickly?

Focus on quantifying achievements in your experience section, ensuring all critical sections are present, removing buzzwords, and tailoring your skills to match the job description.

Q
How accurate is the AI analysis?

We use GPT-4 combined with a fine-tuned skill-matching engine trained on thousands of job descriptions. Our match accuracy for role-specific skills exceeds 90% across major industries.
